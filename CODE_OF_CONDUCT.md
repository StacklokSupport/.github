# Customer Support Community Guidelines

## Our Commitment

At Stacklok, we are committed to providing a respectful and professional support experience for all customers. We pledge to maintain a harassment-free and welcoming support environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment
include:

* Using professional and respectful language
* Being respectful of differing perspectives and technical approaches
* Providing clear and constructive feedback about issues
* Remaining patient while support teams work to resolve your concerns
* Showing courtesy towards support staff and other customers
* Focusing on factual, technical descriptions of issues

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
* Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment of support staff or other customers
* Threatening language or behavior
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
* Repeatedly submitting duplicate support requests or spam
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Our Responsibilities

Stacklok support staff are responsible for upholding these standards and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.
Support staff have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or moderate forum posts, comments, tickets, and other communications that violate these guidelines, or to suspend access temporarily or permanently for customers who engage in behaviors that are deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

These guidelines apply to all Stacklok support channels, including but not limited to: support tickets, community forums, support chat, email communications, and any public spaces where individuals are representing themselves as Stacklok customers. This includes interactions with support staff, other customers, and any communications related to Stacklok products and services.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting our support team at code-of-conduct@stacklok.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. We are committed to maintaining confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
Customers who do not follow these guidelines may face temporary or permanent suspension of support access as determined by Stacklok leadership.
